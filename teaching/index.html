Fieldwork

Basin analysis

Arjan de Leeuw, Yannick Boucharat

1 day out of 5 with master (first year) students to reconstruct the environment of the Vercors (French Alps) carbonate platform.

Mountain building, climate and biodiversity

Arjan de Leeuw, Yannick Boucharat

8 days of fieldwork in the Appenines to reconstruct the history of the Appenines mountain chain, from Eocene to present day, with links to other roll-backs systems in the Mediterranean, basin (de-)connections, climate and evolution of the biosphere.

Sedimentology systems

Arjan de Leeuw, Yannick Boucharat, Anna Duhoux

2 days to reconstruct the environment before and during the Alps’ flexural basin, from upper Jurassic to Pliocene.

Practicals

REEF code practical

Yannick Boucharat

I developped this practical for master 1 and 2 students as an introduction to coral reef terraces dynamics, highlighting the interaction between Quaternary sea-level change, vertical movements and erosion/coral reef growth. The practical is then centered on the utilisation of the REEF code.
